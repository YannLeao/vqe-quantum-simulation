# VQE Quantum Simulation

This repository contains computational experiments developed during an undergraduate research project (PIBIC/FACEPE) focused on quantum simulation methods applied to materials relevant to energy storage systems.

The project explores the use of Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE) algorithms to simulate small molecular systems as a preliminary step toward studying materials relevant to battery technologies.

## Repository Structure

- `src/` – implementation of quantum simulation routines
- `notebooks/` – experimental runs and exploratory simulations
- `data/` – numerical results (CSV files with energy convergence and related metrics)
- `figures/` – plots and figures used in project reports
